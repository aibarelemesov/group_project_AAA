                            Aruzhan



Aruzhan strives for freedom with all her heart.
Any framework and restrictions weigh on her soul,
fettering her movements like shackles. She needs space.
Therefore, choosing between stable well-being and the
undivided right to dispose of herself, she always chooses
the latter. Every time when fate provides Aruzhan with the
opportunity to somehow diversify her life, she without
hesitation give up what is already available in favor of the new,
still unknown. “Overgrowing with moss” is clearly not for her.
She easily go through life, adapting to any circumstances.
The very process of movement is what the soul of Aruzhan longs for.

                            Aibar



He is attracted by beauty and harmony in all forms.
Therefore, the primary basis of Aibar's spiritual
aspirations is the desire to keep them around him.
Therefore, any actions that may result in a violation
of the usual order of things are contrary to nature.
But Aibar will not "fight" with those who are trying
to create such an imbalance. A "thin world" for him
is always "better than a good quarrel," which means that
the enemy should be turned into a friend, showing tact
and diplomacy.
